How to use PropJunkie

Find your own edges in under a minute.
1
Pick a sport and tap a game in the strip to filter the board.
2
Read PropJunkie's projection for each player's prop — it's built from that player's recent games, weighted to recent form.
3
Compare it to the line at your sportsbook. If we project clearly above or below the line, that's your lean.
4
Check recent form (last 5 games) and sample size for context — a thin sample is flagged.
5
Want the full breakdown on one prop? Open the Analyzer to generate a projection and edge against a line you enter.
